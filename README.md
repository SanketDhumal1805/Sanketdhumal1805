### 🔭 About me

I'm an ECE undergrad who ended up spending most of final year in Python instead of a breadboard — building systems that sit at the intersection of **electronics, computer vision, and deep learning**. I like projects that combine a real sensing/data problem with a model that actually has to work outside a Jupyter notebook: a working UI, a database, a report someone can hand to an evaluator.

Two projects anchor that so far:
- A **4-channel ResNet50 that fuses RGB leaf photos with Sentinel-2 satellite NDVI data** to classify citrus plant diseases — my current B.Tech research project.
- An **AI-based face verification attendance system** (OpenCV + `face_recognition` + Firebase), built as a 4-person team project.

I care about being honest about what a model can and can't do — both of the projects below include an explicit "Limitations" section, because a system that overclaims its accuracy is worse than one that's upfront about its edges.
